Corporate Daycare Solutions for Businesses

The challenge of striking a work-life balance and the difficulty of locating dependable, reasonably priced childcare are two of the more difficult issues facing the millennial corporate workforce in India. Corporate Daycare is the one service that can make a difference.

Corporate employees get additional benefits at Kids Cube. Several corporations have supported and encouraged us in our efforts to reach out to concerned and discerning young parents.

Kids Cube provides near-site daycare services to working parents to help them while they are at work.

Features

  • Employees have a completely worry-free Daycare experience. This improves employee engagement and productivity
  • One-point Solution for Playgroup, Nursery, Kindergarten, Day Care, and activity center
  • Children get a safe, caring environment, conducive to learning
  • Little Millennium Preschools are pioneers in the area of Childcare with experience and expertise of 15+ years in engaging and caring for young children

Corporate Benefits to Employees

  • At our 750 locations throughout India, corporate partners enjoy Special Privileges on School & Daycare Admissions.
  • Through our Parent Connect Programs, parents may also take advantage of our years of experience. Programs can be run internally, customized for the comfort of your staff, and included in your efforts to increase employee engagement.

What we offer?

  • Instant Mobile Updates - Track your child's progress and learning through the Kids Cube App
  • Safety & Security - We provide CCTV-enabled campus for the safety and security of your child.
  • Child-friendly Infrastructure - We provide a perfect blend of style, aesthetics, and reliable, child-friendly, and functional infrastructure
  • Transfer Facility - Transfer facilities are available without any added cost in case of transferable jobs of parents pan India.

Faq's

What is the Corporate Tie-Up program at Kids Cube?

The Corporate Tie-Up program is a partnership initiative between Kids Cube and corporate organizations to provide specialized early childhood education and daycare services for employees. The program offers unique advantages, including admission offers, priority placements, and customized daycare solutions, making childcare more accessible and less stressful for working parents.

Who can participate in the Corporate Tie-Up program?

Any corporate organization, whether small, medium, or large, can participate in the program. It’s ideal for companies that want to support their employees with young children by offering quality early education and daycare services.

What are the benefits of partnering with Kids Cube?

Partnering with Kids Cube offers several benefits, including:

  • Exclusive offers on preschool and daycare for employees' children.

  • Flexible daycare options that accommodate working hours.

  • PAN India transfer facility across any Kids Cube center.

  • Employee engagement sessions focused on parenting and early childhood education.

  • Upscale & Value add to company events with child-friendly setups.

  • Improved work-life balance and productivity for employees with young children.

How can we get in touch to discuss a Corporate Tie-Up?

You can connect with our Corporate Partnership team via email at neha.dolwani@littlemillennium.com or call us at 9595082445. Alternatively, you can fill out the contact form on our website, and our team will respond promptly.

Are there any costs associated with the Corporate Tie-Up program?

There are no direct costs for joining the Corporate Tie-Up program. The partnership mainly offers benefits such as special offers and services for employees.

Is there a minimum number of employees required for a Corporate Tie-Up?

No, there’s no minimum employee requirement. Kids Cube can collaborate with companies of all sizes to create customized childcare solutions that meet their employees' needs.

Can we schedule a meeting to discuss potential partnership opportunities?

Yes, you can schedule a meeting with our Corporate Partnership team to explore tailored solutions for your organization. We are available for virtual meetings, in-person visits, or site tours. Please contact us via phone or email to set up an appointment.

Can Kids Cube set up a daycare facility at our corporate office?

Yes, our business partners offer on-site daycare solutions tailored to your office's needs and the space available.
